Jokić began his professional basketball career with Mega Vizura in Serbia before joining the NBA in 2015.

Since then, he has been a consistent All-Star and has won three MVP awards for his incredible performance and leadership on the court.

In 2023, he led the Denver Nuggets to their first-ever NBA Championship and was named Finals MVP.

His style of play has revolutionized the center position, emphasizing skill, passing, and high basketball IQ.
