LeBron James

LeBron James is widely considered one of the greatest basketball players of all time. Known for his combination of size, athleticism, and basketball IQ, he has maintained elite performance for over two decades.

Career Achievements:

  • NBA Championships: 4 (2012, 2013, 2016, 2020)
  • NBA Most Valuable Player (MVP): 4 (2009, 2010, 2012, 2013)
  • NBA Finals MVP: 4 (2012, 2013, 2016, 2020)
  • NBA All-Star Appearances: 20+
  • NBA All-NBA First Team: 13 times
  • NBA All-Defensive First Team: 5 times
  • NBA Rookie of the Year: 2004

All-Time Rankings (as of 2024):

  • Points: 40,000+ (NBA All-Time Leader)
  • Rebounds: 11,000+
  • Assists: 10,000+ (Top 5 All-Time)
  • Triple-Doubles: 100+
  • Field Goals Made: 15,000+
  • Field Goal Percentage: ~50%
  • Three-Point Percentage: ~34%
  • Minutes Played: 60,000+

  • Oldest player to drop 40+ in a game (age 39+)
