Magic Johnson

Magic Johnson played from 1979 to 1991, briefly returning in 1996. In that time period, he amassed Career Achievements:

Career Achievements:

  • NBA Championships: 5 (1980, 1982, 1985, 1987, 1988)
  • NBA Most Valuable Player (MVP) Awards: 3 (1987, 1989, 1990)
  • NBA All-Star Appearances: 12 (1980, 1982–1992)
  • NBA All-Star Game MVP: 2 (1990, 1992)
  • NBA Assists Leader: 4 times (1983, 1984, 1986, 1987)
  • NBA Steals Leader: 1981
  • NBA Finals MVP: 3 (1980, 1982, 1987)

All-Time Rankings:

  • Points: 17,707 – 19.5 PPG
  • Rebounds: 6,559 – 7.2 RPG
  • Assists: 10,141 – 11.2 APG (1st All-Time APG)
  • Field Goals Made: 6,211
  • Field Goal Percentage: 52.0%
  • Minutes Played: 33,245
  • Free Throw Percentage: 84.8%

Single-Game Records:

  • Most Assists in a Game: 24 (career high)
  • Triple-Double in NBA Finals Debut (Game 6, 1980): 42 PTS, 15 REB, 7 AST playing center
  • Youngest NBA Finals MVP (age 20)

Notable Career Feats:

  • Only rookie to win NBA Finals MVP
  • Holds highest career assists-per-game average in NBA history (11.2)
  • Led Lakers to 9 NBA Finals appearances in 12 seasons

Hall of Fame Induction:

  • 2002: Magic Johnson was inducted into the Naismith Memorial Basketball Hall of Fame
