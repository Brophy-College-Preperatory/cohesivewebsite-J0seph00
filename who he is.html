Nikola Jokić, born on February 19, 1995, in Sombor, Serbia, is one of the most talented and unique basketball players in the world today.

He was drafted 41st overall by the Denver Nuggets in the 2014 NBA Draft, a move that would turn out to be one of the greatest steals in league history.

Jokić has become known for his incredible passing, basketball IQ, and versatility, redefining the role of the center in the modern NBA.
